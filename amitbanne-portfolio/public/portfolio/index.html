About Me

I was born and brought up in Hubli, one of the major cities in the South Indian state of Karnataka.

I have completed my entire school education at Chinmaya Vidyalaya, Hubli. I was an active member of the school sports teams, having represented the school in Athletics, Cricket, Soccer.Ever since I represented my school in soccer, I became an arduent soccer follower. I am a die-hard fan of Manchester United.#GGMU

After my schooling, I joined Chetan College, Hubli in 2006 for my pre-university education with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology being my course combination.

After completing my pre-university education in 2008, I got myself enrolled at B.V.Bhoomaraddi College of Engineering and Technology, Hubli for Bachelors in Electronics and Communication Engineering. So far, my entire education had been in Hubli. Towards my third year of bachelors, I started developing fascination towards the field of Computer Science. Towards the end of my third year, I made up my mind to make a switch to the computer science field. I decided to take up a job in the Software industry. I joined some external courses in programming languages to prepare for the same. I was able to bag an offer from Tata Consultancy Services(TCS) in the placements during my final year. I completed my graduation in 2008.

I joined TCS in October 2012. I attended a 3-month training program, known as Initial Learning Program, at TCS - Ahmedabad, Gujarat. This was the first time I was moving out of Hubli. I was awarded with "Initial Learning Program-KUDOS" during the training program for excellent performance. After completion of training, I received my posting as Bangalore and from there on, I worked at Tata Consultancy Services(TCS) Ltd. for the next 2.5 years, where I was able to build my Software Development, Object Oriented Programming and Database skills. I have designed and developed applications in Java and SQL technologies as part of the team. I was able to master the art of being a team player. I have experience of working in the Banking and Retail industry specific applications. I have a good understanding of the Software Development Life-cycle.

After working at TCS for almost 3 years, I felt the hunger to to do more than what I had been doing at TCS. Having been interested in technology, I realized a Master's degree in Computer Science would help me achieve that. I got myself enrolled for the Master of Science program in Computer Science at Northeastern University, Boston in Fall 2015.

So far, it has been an amazing experience. I am grateful to my parents and my friends, who have always been there for me.

  2. LookUp is a simple Android App to Search places like ATMs, pharmacies, restaurants etc that are "Open" in vicinity at the time of searching.

  3. Search can be of two types :
  4. By specifying search radius (E.g.: 0.5, 1, 2 miles etc) and Place Category (E.g.: atm, pharmacy, restaurant etc)
  5. By specifying search radius (E.g.: 0.5, 1, 2 miles etc) and place name(E.g.: CVS, Target, Domino's, Subway etc)

  6. The application displays the places that match search criteria

  7. For each of the places that match the search criteria, following information is displayed
  8. Name of the place
  9. Open/Close Status
  10. Address of the place
  11. Rating of the place
  12. Exact distance of the place (in miles) from the current search location


  13. The application searches places within specified radius around the current location (latitude and longitude accessed from phone directly)

  14. The application fetches data from Google Places API

  15. The JSON data received from Google Places API is parsed and displayed as a list
